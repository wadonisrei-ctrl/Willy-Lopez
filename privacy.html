Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Willy Lopez, licensed real estate agent (Lic. SL3617612) operating under Taylor Apartment Locator ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our website or services.

By submitting a form on this website or contacting us directly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Apartment search preferences: desired area, move-in timeline, budget range, and number of bedrooms
  • Any additional details you share during our communications

We do not collect any information through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools. We do not use third-party advertising networks on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you about your apartment search via phone call, text message, or email
  • Identify apartment communities that match your stated preferences
  • Coordinate tours and assist with the application process
  • Respond to your questions and provide ongoing support during your search

We do not use your information for any purpose unrelated to providing apartment locating services to you.

3. Telephone and Text Message Communications (TCPA)

By submitting your phone number through any form on this website, you expressly consent to receive calls and text messages from Willy Lopez at the number provided, including calls and texts placed using automated dialing systems or prerecorded messages, for the purpose of discussing your apartment search. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or by contacting us directly.

Consent to receive marketing communications is not a condition of using our services. You may reach us by email or phone call without submitting your number through the web form.

4. Information Sharing

We share limited information about your search preferences (such as desired area, budget, and timeline) with apartment communities and their leasing staff as a necessary part of the services we provide. We do not share your full contact information with third parties without your permission.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services to you and for a reasonable period afterward for business record-keeping purposes. If you would like your information deleted from our records, please contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of text message communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications at any time by notifying us directly

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the information listed below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
