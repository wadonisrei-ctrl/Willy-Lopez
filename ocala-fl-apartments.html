Ocala Neighborhoods I Cover

Ocala has distinct neighborhoods with different price ranges and vibes. Here is a quick breakdown:

Silver Springs Shores is the most affordable area in Ocala, popular with working families and budget-conscious renters. Expect rents from $900 to $1,200 for a one-bedroom.

SE Ocala along the 17th Street corridor has newer builds and is popular with healthcare workers from the nearby hospitals. Rents run $1,100 to $1,500.

Downtown Ocala has gone through a revival over the last several years. Walkable, with local restaurants and coffee shops. Good for younger professionals. Rents around $1,200 to $1,600.

Paddock Park and NW Ocala round out the market with a mix of price points from $950 to $1,800 depending on the community.

I can match you to the neighborhood that fits your commute, budget, and lifestyle.
